Our History

Our organization began its journey over 30 years ago with a small group of dedicated individuals who were passionate about serving the community. The initial vision was simple: to create a space where people could come together, share their faith, and support one another through life's challenges.

In our early years, we held gatherings in rented spaces and homes, which quickly grew as more people joined our mission. Over the decades, we've established various programs, including education, healthcare, and mentorship initiatives, to address the needs of our community.
